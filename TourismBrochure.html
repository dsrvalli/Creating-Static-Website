Tourism

Perched on the very happening Colva Beach, Beleza By The Beach is one of the most family-friendly beach resorts in Goa India. Spread across an area of around 5 acres, the resort features an alluring amalgamation of contemporary and traditional Goan architectural design, with its villas and rooms showcasing modern amenities. Beleza By The Beach flaunts excellent culinary arrangements that include Tentacao, their multi-cuisine all-day dining poolside restaurant and bar and Nazare, their beach shack and bar. The resort also makes available all kinds of invigorating body massage services at Espaco Divino, their in-house spa facility.
